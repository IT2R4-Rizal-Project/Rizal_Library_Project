Lesson 2: Jose Rizal and Philippine Nationalism: Bayani and Kabayanihan

Introduction

José Rizal's concept of bayani (hero) and kabayanihan (heroism) played a crucial role in shaping Philippine nationalism. This lesson explores how Rizal's understanding of heroism influenced the development of Filipino national identity and continues to inspire modern notions of patriotism.
