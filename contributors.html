Course Contributors

Meet the teams behind our comprehensive course materials

Chapter Teams

Our course materials are developed by dedicated teams of students, each focusing on specific chapters of Rizal's life and works:

  • Chapter 1: Rizal's Family and Early Life
  • Chapter 2: Rizal's Education and Travels
  • Chapter 3: Rizal's Literary Works
  • Chapter 4: Rizal's Last Days and Execution
  • Chapter 5: Rizal's Writings and Nationalism

Team Members

Our teams consist of dedicated students and researchers who contribute to various aspects of the course materials:

  • Content Researchers
  • Writers and Editors
  • Technical Contributors
  • Educational Consultants
  • Peer Reviewers
